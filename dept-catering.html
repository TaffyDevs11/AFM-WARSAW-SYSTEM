Welcome Message

A Word from Our Leader

Welcome to the AFM Warsaw Catering Department — where love is cooked into every meal and hospitality is our spiritual act of worship to God and His people.

We believe that feeding people is a ministry. From large church events to intimate fellowships, our catering team serves with warmth, joy, and culinary excellence — nourishing both body and spirit.

We look forward to having you with us. Come and be part of something truly extraordinary — because together, we are unstoppable in God!

Sis. Agnes Phiri
Our Story

About the Catering

✦
🎯
Our Purpose
To serve the body of Christ with excellent, joyful, and Spirit-led hospitality through food preparation and catering at all church events, ensuring every guest feels welcomed and cared for.
📅
How We Gather
Serving at all major church events, Sunday lunches, conventions, outreach programs, and coordinating food donations for community welfare initiatives.
🌍
Our Impact
Church event catering, community food outreach, training in food safety and hospitality, and creating an atmosphere of warmth and welcome at every gathering.
