Our Story

Rooted in Faith,
Growing in Purpose

✦

AFM Warsaw Assembly — Warsaw Christian Centre is a vibrant, gospel-centred church family committed to the advancement of the Kingdom of God in Poland and beyond. We believe that every person is created for a divine purpose, and our mission is to help individuals discover and fulfil that calling.

Through dynamic worship, transformative preaching, Spirit-led prayer, and authentic community, we pursue God's heart and extend His love to Warsaw.
