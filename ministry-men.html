Welcome Message

A Word from Our Leader

"Welcome, brothers, to a community of men standing firm in God."

Brothers, I want to personally welcome you to the AFM Warsaw Men's Ministry — a brotherhood forged in faith, shaped by the Word, and sustained by the Holy Spirit. Here, we do not merely gather; we are transformed.

Whether you are a new believer finding your footing, a seasoned Christian looking for accountability, or a man searching for meaning and direction — this is your community. We are committed to raising men who are strong in character, faithful in family, and fearless in purpose.

Come as you are, but do not stay as you are. We believe God has extraordinary plans for every man in this fellowship.

Bro. David Chikamhi

About the Men's Ministry

✦
🎯
Our Purpose
We exist to raise men who are anchored in Scripture, grounded in prayer, and mobilised to serve their families, church, and community with godly integrity.
📅
Our Gatherings
Monthly brotherhood meetings, quarterly retreats, weekly prayer groups, and annual men's conference. We create spaces for authentic fellowship and spiritual growth.
🌍
Our Impact
Community outreach, family strengthening initiatives, mentorship programs, and active participation in church growth and gospel advancement throughout Warsaw.
