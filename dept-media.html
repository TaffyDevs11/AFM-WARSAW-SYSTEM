Welcome Message

A Word from Our Leader

Welcome to the technical heartbeat of AFM Warsaw Assembly — Sound & Media: where excellence meets service for the kingdom of God.

Our Sound & Media team ensures that every word preached, every song sung, every message delivered — online and in person — reaches ears with the clarity and quality it deserves. We serve behind the scenes so the gospel can shine front and centre.

We look forward to having you with us. Come and be part of something truly extraordinary — because together, we are unstoppable in God!

Bro. Emmanuel Mwale
